Our services

At 2MA Petroleum, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet the energy needs of industries, businesses, and individuals. With our expertise and commitment to quality, we ensure solutions tailored to market demands.




1. Supply and Distribution of Oil Products

Supply and distribute various petroleum products, including gasoline, diesel, fuel oil, bitumen and base oils. Thanks to an efficient logistics network and strategic partnerships, we guarantee fast and secure delivery, respecting the highest quality and environmental standards.

2. Renewable Energy Solutions

Committed to the energy transition, we offer solutions adapted to the needs of companies and industries wishing to integrate clean energy into their activities. Our services include the installation of solar panels, infrastructure maintenance and energy efficiency advice for more responsible and sustainable consumption.

3. Energy Storage and Logistics

We implement optimized storage and transportation solutions to ensure a continuous and secure energy supply. Our modern infrastructure and logistics expertise ensure the optimal storage of petroleum products and their efficient distribution on a large scale.

4. B2B Distribution

We cater to the needs of businesses and industries by supplying fuels and other petroleum products in bulk volumes. Our B2B distribution model ensures efficient supply chain management with tailored solutions for each sector, guaranteeing reliability and profitability.

5. Distribution via Mobile Stations

To facilitate access to fuels and petroleum products in remote or high-demand areas, we offer fully equipped mobile stations. These flexible infrastructures enable the safe refueling of vehicles, machinery, and industrial equipment without requiring a fixed station.

6. Construction of Petroleum Infrastructure

We design and build petroleum infrastructures tailored to our clients' needs, including storage depots, service stations, pipelines, and refining units. Our projects adhere to strict safety and environmental standards, ensuring durability and optimal performance for seamless operations.

7. Inventory Management

We provide advanced inventory management solutions to optimize fuel and energy product supply. With real-time tracking technologies, we ensure precise control of available volumes, reducing the risks of shortages and minimizing storage costs.

8. Depot Management

We oversee the operation and management of petroleum depots, ensuring compliance with safety, quality, and regulatory standards. Our teams supervise storage, transfer, and distribution operations to maintain a smooth and efficient supply chain.
