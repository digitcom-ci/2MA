About us

Welcome to 2MA Petroleum

Welcome to 2MA Petroleum, a petroleum trading company registered in Dubai and in Côte d’Ivoire. We aim to become your trusted partner in the oil & Gas sector. Specializing in the trading of petroleum products in Africa and across the world, we leverage our expertise to serve industries, businesses, and individuals. With a sustainable and innovative approach, we are committed to providing reliable supply and trading solutions while adhering to the strictest environmental standards.
